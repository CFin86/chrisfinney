trimr

The Overview

trimr started as final project during the Covalence boot camp. It is Software as a Service responsive mobile web site built with Bootstrap and AngularJS. It queries a MySQL database with Node.js and calls to a third-party API. Users are able to connect to mobile hairstylists via the Twilio and Appointlet API, where they will come to your location to cut your hair. During our presentation I booked an appointment with a stylist live and had her cut my hair! Currently, we are pursuing startup funding. A short demonstration can be viewed here.
